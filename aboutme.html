About Me

Designer & Mom

Hi all, my name is Alyssa and I am a mom and a full time worker and designer. I love making high quality hand made products for everyone!I love experiementing with new mediums for the design I create. I am also a disney fanatic so I am always happy to make any custom disney products. My speciality is in t-shirt and cup/tumblr designs. I am always happy to fulfil any requests big or small. Just send me an email with what you would like and I will do my best to get it perfect for you. I am hoping one day I can make designs full time so supporting my business means the world to me! Thank you so much for visiting my page and please let me know if there is anything I can do to provide the best products for you :)
